San Antonio Health Literacy Coalition

The San Antonio Health Literacy Initiative (SAHLI) is a comprehensive program that aims to increase health literacy awareness and efficacy through strategically addressing primary gaps in health literacy today by...

  1. Providing local and relevant information as to what health literacy means and how it applies to daily life activities and health awareness
  2. Modifying provider practices to ensure proper and effective patient-health provider communication histories
  3. Instituting cultural change towards health literacy through empowering patients and health providers to participate in a more health literate society

Bexar Healthy Beverage Coalition

The Bexar Healthy Beverage Coalition (BHBC) strives to raise awareness and educate the community on just how much sugar is in the beverages we drink, as well as offer suggestions on how to pick healthier choices. Here in San Antonio and South Texas, thousands of families struggle with the twin epidemics of obesity and diabetes. Experts say that sugar-packed drinks contribute to these problems. They also raise the risk of heart disease, tooth and gum disease and more.

Immunization Collaborative

The mission of the Immunize San Antonio (IZSA) coalition is to promote immunizations through collaboration and education. Our vision is a community where all residents are protected from vaccine preventable diseases. The collaborative comes together every second Thursday of the month from 12:30pm - 1:30pm to discuss the issues and opportunities in regards to immunizations in Bexar County. IZSA assists other partners and attends community events that surround immuniations as well as helps to find partners for events that need an immunization provider.
